# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Bilingual professionals (Korean/English) searching jobs across US and South Korea markets. They use this during their workday at a desk, researching their next career move.

## Product Purpose
Search and filter thousands of job postings across multiple sources (Saramin, Wanted, LinkedIn, etc.) with bilingual support. Success: a user finds relevant jobs fast and applies.

## Brand Personality
Crisp, fast, minimal. Linear DNA without the keyboard fanaticism. The tool disappears into the task.

## Anti-references
- LinkedIn/Indeed: busy, ad-heavy, information-dense, cluttered
- SaaS landing templates: gradient heroes, feature grids, testimonial cards
- Observability dashboards: dark navy, neon accents, terminal aesthetic

## Design Principles
1. Speed is UX. Every interaction should feel instant. No unnecessary animation, no wait states.
2. Content first, chrome second. Job listings are the product. Navigation and filters serve them, not the other way.
3. Bilingual by default. Every label, filter, and state must work for EN and KR text lengths.
4. Honest data. Stats and counts are useful but not decorative. Show them where they help decisions, not to fill space.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA plus full keyboard navigation for search and filter interactions.
